Our Affiliates

The Sangguniang Kabataan collaborates with various organizations and institutions to enhance our programs and initiatives for the youth. Here are some of our key affiliates:

  • Department of Youth and Sports Development

    The Department of Youth and Sports Development supports SK in organizing youth sports events and training programs to foster a healthy and active lifestyle among young people.

  • Local Government Units

    We work closely with various local government units to align our projects with community needs and ensure effective implementation of youth programs at the barangay level.

  • Non-Governmental Organizations (NGOs)

    NGOs such as Youth for Change and Community Builders collaborate with us to provide additional resources, training, and advocacy for youth empowerment and development.
