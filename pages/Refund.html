Why Refunds are Important for SK Projects

In the context of Sangguniang Kabataan (SK) projects, refunds ensure that funds are used appropriately. If a project or activity does not go as planned, refunds can help recover funds for reallocation or to address any issues.

How to Request a Refund

To request a refund for SK projects, follow these steps:

  1. Contact the relevant SK officer or committee.
  2. Provide details about the expenditure and reason for the refund.
  3. Submit any required documents or proof related to the expense.
  4. Follow up to ensure the refund process is completed.

Refund Policies for SK

SK may have specific policies regarding refunds, which may include:

  • Timeframe for refund requests
  • Documentation required

Always check with your SK officers to understand the specific policies that apply to your situation.
